About Us

Building a community of women in tech at Calvin University

Our Mission

Girls Who Code College Loop at Calvin University is dedicated to closing the gender gap in technology. We provide a supportive community where women can learn, grow, and excel in computer science and related fields.

What We Do

  • Weekly coding workshops and tutorials
  • Networking events with industry professionals
  • Collaborative coding projects
  • Career development and mentorship opportunities
  • Community building and peer support
